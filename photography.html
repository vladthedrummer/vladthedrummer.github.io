Sometimes in life you hit your lowest points and you try to find things that can distract your mind from the bad. You try everything you know and when all else fails you try something new. I went out and bought the cheapest DSLR camera I could buy and then started tinkering with the camera. At first everything was done at the cameras auto mode; it was fun but I wanted to try to get the most of the camera. I would search online about how to step outside of auto and go into aperture, shutter speed, and manual modes.

I found expression in a new way through my eyes through the lens. It was peaceful and there is a huge learning curve but I am always one for a challenge. I continually want to improve and grow because I feel like I am my biggest critic when it comes to taking my own photos. I am learning to love the flaws in respect to my photos because otherwise I would be the same as everyone else.
