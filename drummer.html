When you are a kid and hit pots and pans with spoons you know for a fact that you are having the time of your life making noise just to annoy your mom. For me it has always been fun but I never had the chance to play drums since most of my life I have lived in a one bedroom apartment. Also, in Chicago most kids do not touch an instrument until they get to high school like myself. The thing is I did not learn in school as a matter of fact I have never had lessons.

I would just watch YouTube videos and just mimic what my favorite drummers would be doing. I auditioned for a band in high school without ever playing the drums in my life and I got the spot. None, of my band members knew that; I had lied to them about drumming for years. Ever since then I have been trying to become a better drummer and just trying develop myself as an established drummer.
